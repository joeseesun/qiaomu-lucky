# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be direct, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and specific in feedback.
- Assume good intent, but keep technical claims evidence-based.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing reproduction steps.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discrimination.
- Publishing private credentials, personal data, or prize codes.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or low-effort issue flooding.
- Requests to add gambling, betting, or unlawful campaign features.

Maintainers may close issues, edit comments, or block participants who ignore these expectations.
